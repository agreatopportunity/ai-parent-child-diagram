🚀 Key Benefits of Satellite-Ground AI Networks

This distributed parent-child model enables global AI deployment, redundant communication pathways, and resilient emergency systems. The satellite constellation provides global coverage and high-capacity processing, while ground stations offer local optimization and human oversight. Ham radio integration ensures communication resilience during disasters or system failures.

📡 Technical Implementation

Uplink/Downlink: Ka/Ku band for high-speed data • Ham Radio: VHF/UHF emergency backup • Processing: Edge AI on satellites with ground station optimization • Protocols: Software-defined radio with AI-optimized packet routing
